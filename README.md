# About Me:
I’m currently completing a Master’s degree in Advanced Artificial Intelligence, with a strong focus on machine learning, computer vision, and time series modeling. I have over five years of experience building and evaluating ML pipelines using pose estimation data for human movement recognition and quality analysis. Alongside my research work, I’ve developed several open-source React projects and enjoy developing end-to-end systems that bridge research, data, and production.

<br>Feel free to reach out to me to talk about data!!!
